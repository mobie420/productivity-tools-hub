Why Browser Extensions?

Your browser is where you spend most of your day. A few strategic extensions can eliminate repetitive tasks and keep you focused.

The Ultimate List

📚 Research & Reading

  1. Reader Mode – Strips clutter from articles.
  2. Save to Notion – Saves web pages directly to Notion.
  3. Zotero Connector – Captures citations with one click.

⚙️ Automation

  1. Text Blaze – Text expansion for common replies.
  2. Auto‑Text Expander – Another great text expander.
  3. IFTTT – Connects web services automatically.

🎯 Focus & Blocking

  1. Forest – Plant a tree for every focus session.
  2. StayFocusd – Limits time‑wasting sites.
  3. BlockSite – Blocks distracting websites.

📊 Productivity & Workflow

  1. Toggl Track – One‑click time tracking.
  2. Momentum – Beautiful new‑tab dashboard.
  3. Workona – Tab and workspace manager.

✨ Design & Development

  1. ColorZilla – Eyedropper and color picker.
  2. WhatFont – Identifies fonts on any site.
  3. JSON Formatter – Pretty‑prints JSON.

🔍 Search & Navigation

  1. Search by Image – Reverse image search.
  2. OneTab – Converts tabs into a list.
  3. The Great Suspender – Suspends inactive tabs.

📨 Email & Communication

  1. Grammarly – Real‑time grammar checking.
  2. Boomerang for Gmail – Schedule emails.
  3. Mailtrack – Email tracking.

🛒 Shopping & Deals

  1. Honey – Automatically applies coupon codes.
  2. Keepa – Amazon price history.
  3. Camelizer – Price drop alerts.

🔒 Privacy & Security

  1. uBlock Origin – Efficient ad blocker.
  2. Privacy Badger – Blocks invisible trackers.
  3. HTTPS Everywhere – Forces secure connections.

How to Install

  1. Open the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Search for the extension name.
  3. Click “Add to Chrome.”
  4. Pin the extension to your toolbar for easy access.

  1. uBlock Origin – Faster, cleaner browsing.
  2. Text Blaze – Saves hours of typing.
  3. Forest – Keeps you accountable.
  4. Save to Notion – Centralizes your research.
  5. Toggl Track – Know where your time goes.
