What Is Time‑Blocking?

Time‑blocking is the practice of scheduling every hour of your day, turning your calendar into a productivity engine.

How to Start Time‑Blocking Today

  1. Audit your week: Track where your time actually goes.
  2. Block non‑negotiables: Sleep, meals, exercise.
  3. Schedule deep work: 2‑3 hour blocks for your most important tasks.
  4. Leave buffers: 30‑minute gaps between blocks.
  5. Review weekly: Adjust based on what worked.
