## The Email Problem

The average knowledge worker spends 3.1 hours daily on email. These systems can cut that to 30 minutes.

## 1. **The PARA Email Method**

**How it works:** Process emails into Projects, Areas, Resources, or Archives.

**Implementation:**
- **P (Projects):** Email → Todoist/Asana with due date
- **A (Areas):** Label with area (Finance, Health, etc.)
- **R (Resources):** Save to Notion/Evernote
- **A (Archives):** Archive immediately if just FYI

## Daily Email Routine (15 Minutes)

**1. Triage (5 min):**  
- Use Superhuman/SaneBox to filter  
- Apply PARA method  
- Archive/delete immediately

**2. Process (5 min):**  
- Convert to tasks (Todoist)  
- Schedule responses (Boomerang)  
- File reference material (Notion)

**3. Send (5 min):**  
- Batch compose replies  
- Use templates for common responses  
- Schedule for optimal delivery times

## Weekly Maintenance (30 Minutes Friday)

1. **Unsubscribe** from 5 new newsletters (Clean Email)
2. **Review** SaneBox filters for accuracy
3. **Archive** completed project emails
4. **Update** email templates based on frequent replies
