## The Remote Communication Stack

Effective remote work requires more than just Zoom. Here's the complete toolkit.

## 1. **Slack** – The Async Hub

**What it does:** Channel‑based messaging with deep integrations.

**Best for:** Day‑to‑day team communication and quick questions.

**Pricing:** Free for limited history; Pro $8.75/user/month.

**Why we recommend it:** Still the gold standard for team chat.

## 2. **Loom** – Async Video

**What it does:** Record quick screen‑and‑camera videos instead of scheduling meetings.

**Best for:** Explanations, updates, and feedback that don't need real‑time conversation.

**Pricing:** Free for 25 videos; Business $12.50/user/month.

**Why we recommend it:** Reduces meeting overload by 30‑40%.

[**Get Loom**](https://www.loom.com) *(affiliate link)*

## 3. **Miro** – Virtual Whiteboard

**What it does:** Collaborative infinite canvas for brainstorming, planning, and workshops.

**Best for:** Creative teams, strategists, and workshop facilitators.

**Pricing:** Free for 3 boards; Team $8/user/month.

**Why we recommend it:** The most flexible visual collaboration platform.

[**Try Miro**](https://miro.com) *(affiliate link)*

## 4. **Range** – Daily Check‑ins

**What it does:** Replaces stand‑ups with async check‑ins and mood tracking.

**Best for:** Teams that want fewer meetings but more visibility.

**Pricing:** Free for up to 12 users; Team $8/user/month.

**Why we recommend it:** Beautiful interface, integrates with Slack/GitHub/Jira.

[**Get Range**](https://www.range.co) *(affiliate link)*

## 5. **Figma** – Design Collaboration

**What it does:** Real‑time design and prototyping with commenting and version control.

**Best for:** Design teams and anyone who creates visual content.

**Pricing:** Free for 3 files; Professional $12/editor/month.

**What it does:** All‑in‑one workspace for notes, docs, wikis, and databases.

**Why we recommend it:** Replaces Google Docs, Confluence, and Trello in one tool.

**What it does:** AI‑powered noise cancellation for any microphone.

**Best for:** Teams working in noisy environments or from coffee shops.

**Pricing:** Free for 60 minutes/week; Pro $8/month.

**Small team (2‑10):** Slack + Loom + Notion

1. **Default to async:** Use Loom/Range instead of meetings.
