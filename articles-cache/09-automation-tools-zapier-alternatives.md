## The Automation Landscape

Zapier dominates, but it's not the only option. Here's when to choose each platform.

## 1. **Make (formerly Integromat)** – Visual Powerhouse

**Best for:** Complex workflows with conditional logic and data transformation.

**Key features:**  
- Visual scenario builder with routers and filters  
- Built‑in data stores and functions  
- Error handling and rollback

**Pricing:** Free for 1,000 ops/month; paid from $9/month.

**Why choose it:** More powerful than Zapier for multi‑step workflows.

[**Try Make**](https://www.make.com) *(affiliate link)*

## 2. **n8n** – Self‑Hosted Open Source

**Best for:** Developers and privacy‑conscious teams.

**Key features:**  
- Self‑host on your own infrastructure  
- 200+ built‑in nodes  
- JavaScript/TypeScript customization

**Pricing:** Free self‑hosted; Cloud from $20/month.

**Why choose it:** Complete control and no data leaving your servers.

[**Try n8n**](https://n8n.io) *(affiliate link)*

## 3. **IFTTT** – Consumer‑Focused Simplicity

**Best for:** Personal automation and smart home integration.

**Key features:**  
- Simple "if this then that" interface  
- 700+ services including smart devices  
- Applets community

**Pricing:** Free for 5 applets; Pro $3.99/month.

**Why choose it:** Easiest way to connect smart home and personal apps.

[**Try IFTTT**](https://ifttt.com) *(affiliate link)*

## 4. **Pipedream** – Developer‑First

**Best for:** Developers who want code‑first automation.

**Key features:**  
- Node.js/Python workflows  
- Built‑in test environment  
- HTTP endpoints and cron jobs

**Pricing:** Free for 10,000 invocations/day; paid from $19/month.

**Why choose it:** Write custom code without managing infrastructure.

[**Try Pipedream**](https://pipedream.com) *(affiliate link)*

## 5. **Tray.io** – Enterprise‑Grade

**Best for:** Large organizations with complex integration needs.

**Key features:**  
- Enterprise security and compliance  
- Workflow templates for common business processes  
- Team collaboration features

**Pricing:** Contact sales (starts around $1,200/month).

**Why choose it:** When you need SOC2, HIPAA, or enterprise support.

[**Try Tray.io**](https://tray.io) *(affiliate link)*

## 6. **Zapier** – The Default Choice

**Best for:** Most teams needing reliable, easy‑to‑use automation.

**Key features:**  
- 5,000+ app integrations  
- Simple interface  
- Reliable execution

**Pricing:** Free for 100 tasks/month; paid from $19.99/month.

**Why choose it:** When you just need things to work without complexity.

[**Try Zapier**](https://zapier.com) *(affiliate link)*

## Decision Framework

**Choose Make if:** You need advanced logic and data transformation.  
**Choose n8n if:** You're technical and want self‑hosted/open source.  
**Choose IFTTT if:** It's for personal/smart home use.  
**Choose Pipedream if:** You're a developer who wants code control.  
**Choose Tray.io if:** You're an enterprise with compliance needs.  
**Choose Zapier if:** You want the easiest, most supported option.
