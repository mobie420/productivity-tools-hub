## Sample 30‑Minute Tech‑Enhanced Routine

**6:30 AM:** Wake‑up light begins sunrise simulation  
**6:45 AM:** Alarm sounds gently, check Oura readiness score  
**6:50 AM:** 10‑min Headspace meditation  
**7:00 AM:** Review day on Google Nest Hub (weather, calendar)  
**7:05 AM:** Log breakfast in MyFitnessPal  
**7:10 AM:** Plan day in Todoist (3 most important tasks)  
**7:15 AM:** Begin work with clear mind and priorities

## Pro Tips

- **No phone for first 30 minutes:** Use dedicated devices instead.
- **Consistency > perfection:** Even 70% adherence yields 90% of benefits.
- **Weekly review:** Adjust based on Oura/RescueTime data.
