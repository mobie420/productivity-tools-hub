## The Keyboard‑First Philosophy

Every time your hand leaves the keyboard for the mouse, you lose 2‑3 seconds. Multiply that by hundreds of times daily.

## Universal Shortcuts (Work Everywhere)

**Ctrl/Cmd + T** – New tab  
**Ctrl/Cmd + W** – Close tab/window  
**Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + T** – Reopen closed tab  
**Alt + Tab (Cmd + Tab)** – Switch applications  
**Ctrl/Cmd + L** – Jump to address bar/search  
**F2** – Rename file  
**F5** – Refresh

## Browser Shortcuts (Chrome/Firefox)

**Ctrl/Cmd + 1‑8** – Jump to tab 1‑8  
**Ctrl/Cmd + 9** – Last tab  
**Ctrl/Cmd + Tab** – Next tab  
**Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Tab** – Previous tab  
**Space** – Page down  
**Shift + Space** – Page up  
**Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + B** – Toggle bookmarks bar

## Text Editing (Everywhere Text Works)

**Ctrl/Cmd + Left/Right Arrow** – Jump by word  
**Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Left/Right Arrow** – Select by word  
**Home/End** – Beginning/end of line  
**Ctrl/Cmd + Home/End** – Beginning/end of document  
**Ctrl/Cmd + Backspace** – Delete previous word  
**Ctrl/Cmd + Delete** – Delete next word

## Windows‑Specific Power Moves

**Win + D** – Show desktop  
**Win + E** – File Explorer  
**Win + L** – Lock computer  
**Win + V** – Clipboard history  
**Win + . (period)** – Emoji picker  
**Win + Shift + S** – Screenshot tool

## Mac‑Specific Power Moves

**Cmd + Space** – Spotlight search  
**Cmd + Option + Space** – Advanced Spotlight  
**Cmd + Shift + 3** – Full screenshot  
**Cmd + Shift + 4** – Area screenshot  
**Cmd + Shift + 5** – Screenshot toolbar  
**Cmd + Control + Space** – Character viewer
