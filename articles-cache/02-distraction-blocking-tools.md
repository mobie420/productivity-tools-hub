## Implementation Strategy

1. **Track first:** Use RescueTime for 1 week to identify your biggest time‑wasters.
2. **Start gentle:** Try Forest or Focusmate for light accountability.
3. **Go nuclear:** If gentle doesn’t work, implement Freedom or Cold Turkey.
4. **Schedule blocks:** Don’t block 24/7 – schedule focused hours and allow breaks.

## The Psychology of Blocking

- **Implementation intention:** "When I open my browser during work hours, I will be redirected to my task list."
- **Friction increase:** Adding even 10 seconds of delay reduces distraction frequency by 80%.
- **Environment design:** Make good behavior easy and bad behavior hard.
