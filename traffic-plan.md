# Traffic & Promotion Plan

## Goal
Drive targeted traffic to the affiliate site (https://mobie420.github.io/productivity-tools-hub/) to generate clicks and sales.

## Immediate Actions (Week 1)

### 1. Social Media Posts
- **Twitter/X**: Post a thread about "Top 7 AI Productivity Tools That Actually Save Time" with link to article.
- **LinkedIn**: Share as a professional tip article.
- **Reddit**: Share in relevant subreddits (r/productivity, r/technology, r/software) following community rules.
- **Facebook Groups**: Join productivity/tech groups and share value.

### 2. Forum Participation
- **Hacker News**: Submit article (be careful not to spam).
- **Indie Hackers**: Share in "ship" section.
- **Product Hunt**: Prepare a proper launch with screenshots.

### 3. SEO Optimization
- Ensure each article has meta title, description, and appropriate heading structure.
- Use internal linking between articles.
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console.

### 4. Email Outreach
- If you have an email list, send a curated newsletter featuring the new articles.
- Consider reaching out to productivity bloggers for backlinks.

### 5. Content Syndication
- Republish articles on Medium (with canonical link to original).
- Share snippets on Dev.to, Hashnode.

## Medium‑Term (Month 1)

### 1. Guest Posting
- Write guest posts for productivity blogs with link back to site.

### 2. Social Advertising
- Consider low‑budget Twitter/LinkedIn ads targeting productivity keywords.

### 3. Affiliate Outreach
- Reach out to tool vendors featured in articles; ask for co‑promotion.

### 4. Build an Email List
- Add a simple ConvertKit form to capture emails (already configured in affiliate‑map.json).

## Tracking & Metrics

- Monitor Google Analytics (once set up) for traffic sources.
- Track affiliate clicks via Amazon Associates, ShareASale, and CJ.com dashboards.
- Use UTM parameters for social links.

## Weekly Routine
- Every Sunday: publish new article (automated).
- Monday: promote that article across channels.
- Wednesday: engage with comments/forums.
- Friday: review traffic and adjust.

*This plan is a living document. Update as you test what works.*
